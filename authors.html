Famous Fantasy Authors

J. R. R. Tolkien

  • Author of The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings
  • Lived 1892-1973
  • Wrote high fantasy and is often credited with repopularizing the genre

Lord Dunsany

  • Author of The King of Elfland's Daughter
  • Lived 1878 - 1957
  • Published over 90 books and was an accomplished playwright

J. K. Rowling

  • Author of the Harry Potter series
  • Born 1965
  • UK's best-selling living author
