Famous Tabletop Games

Chainmail

  • Miniature Wargame precursor to Dungeons and Dragons
  • Published in 1971
  • The first ever comercially available fantasy wargame

Dungeons and Dragons

  • The original Role-playing game
  • Created by Gary Gygax, creator of Chainmail
  • Influenced by many fantasy authors and inspired many more

Warhammer Fantasy

  • Tabletop wargame published in 1983
  • Origin of the Warhammer Fantasy setting
  • Led to the creation of many other games set within the same universe

Other Works

  • Magic: the Gathering
  • Pendragon
  • Dungeonworld
  • Vampire: The Masquerade
