The Fantasy Genre

What is Fantasy?

Fantasy is arguably the oldest genre of literature. It involves settings not based on reality, but instead created from the authors imagination. Most fantasy stories feature supernatural elements, such as magic and monsters, but it is not a requirement. When most people think fantasy, they probably think of books, but almost any form of media can be fantasy.

Throughout this site, you will find information about fantasy books, games, movies and anything else that you might want to know about what is one of the most fun and interesting genres.

Subgenres

  • High Fantasy
  • Low Fantasy
  • Sword and Sorcery
  • Epic Fantasy
