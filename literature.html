Famous Fantasy Literature

The oldest known piece of writing is actually a work of fantasy. The books below were all highly influential in one way or another.

Lord of the Rings

  • Over 150 million copies sold
  • Published 1954
  • Hugely influential to Fantasy Genre

Wizard of Oz

  • Published in 1900
  • Considered the first American Fairy Tale
  • First book in a long series

Le Morte D'Arthur

  • A comprehensive collection of King Arthur Legends
  • Published 1485
  • Written by Sir Thomas Malory

Other Works

  • The Epic of Gilgamesh
  • American Gods
  • Eragon
  • A Game of Thrones
