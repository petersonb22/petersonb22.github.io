Notable Fantasy Video Games

Colossal Cave Adventure

  • Earliest text based adventure video game
  • Heavily inspired by Dungeons and Dragons
  • Inspiration for games like Zork and other interactive fiction

The Legend of Zelda

  • First game released in 1986 for the NES
  • Over 20 games in the series
  • Widely considered to be one of the greatest video game series

The Witcher

  • Based on a popular series of Polish fantasy stories
  • Most recent game considered one of the best fantasy RPGs
  • Noted for morally grey gameplay decisions
